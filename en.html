About Me

IT enthusiast since the first time I used a computer in the 2000s, programmer and gamer in spare time.

Always in search of knowledge

My career in the world of technology began since my childhood, my first experience in the computer world was to a white Pentium 4, I remember as if it were today, all the games I played and the total fun contained in that white box.

My career as a programmer began when I entered my first course focused on the computer area. From this moment on I fell in love with programming, and its extremely vast and broad world, with new ideas and solutions being created at all times. This world fascinates me every day and drives me to think outside the box at every moment of my trajectory.

Due to this interest I entered the course of Systems Analysis and Development at the IFSP ​ (Federal Institute of São Paulo). I totally dedicated myself to studies, seeking to learn the highest standard of software development, since software creation architecture until application production deployment.
